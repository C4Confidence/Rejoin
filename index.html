Key Features

Login

The login page allows users to access their personalized REJOIN dashboard. By logging in, users can apply for jobs, search internships, connect with mentors, and track application progress. This secure page ensures your information is protected and your experience is customized to your career goals.

Vacancies

The vacancies page lists current job openings tailored for recent graduates. Here, users can explore various positions across industries, apply directly through the app, and save opportunities for later. This section is regularly updated to provide the latest job listings, helping users find the perfect start to their careers.

Careers

The careers page provides resources and guidance for building a successful career. Users can find articles, tips, and advice on resume writing, interview preparation, and professional development. This section is designed to help recent graduates navigate their career paths, gain industry insights, and achieve their professional goals.

About REJOIN

Welcome to REJOIN, the bridge between academic achievement and professional success. Our app is designed with recent graduates in mind, aiming to connect you with job opportunities and internships that will set the stage for a thriving career. We understand that transitioning from academia into the workforce can be daunting, which is why REJOIN is here to facilitate a smooth and seamless entry into your professional journey.

REJOIN is also a portfolio project for ALX Software Engineering Program. You can check out the full project on GitHub.
